FAQ

How do Marshalls view how many events a student has enrolled in?

Marshalls have to click on the students button and press the view buttons on each row to view how many events and which events the selected student is enrolled in.

How do Marshalls change and edit the heats and the students participating in each event?

Marshalls have to click on the Events button and remove the students from the heats.
To add, edit or remove a student, press the buttons on the right to edit each heat, use the dropdown box to change events and view the participants in each event
